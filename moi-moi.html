Moi-Moi

Description

This is a delicious, Moi-Moi i love it so much especially when its hot and eaten with soft bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of beans
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Egg
  • Turning stick
  • Bowl
  • Packaged Nylon/Tin

Steps

  1. Soak the beans inside water and wait for some minutes like 5-10 minutes
  2. Peal the beans shell by rubbing it on your palm until every bean shells is removed
  3. Pour the water from the beans and make sure every shell is out then pour another clean water to verify every bean shells has been removed
  4. Add the Pepper and Onions Then Gring it together with the Grinding machine
  5. After Grinding make sure its smooth and soft and pour it in a bowl then start using the Turning stick to turn it
  6. Add Maggi and Salt to it and onions
  7. Boil your Eggs and after its done peal the shell and its either you cut it into two half or you can decide to use it in one piece
  8. After adding the Maggi and salt wuth Onions and you keep turning then you take the package nylon/tin and pour the Beans we have grind and added the ingredients inside it don't forget to add the egg before sealing the Packaging Nylon/Tin
  9. Take a big Pot that can contaion the amount of Packaging Nylon/Tin that is used to Package the moi moi and pour enough amount of water and light our cooker and place is on
  10. Wait for 10-20 minutes to checn on it if there is no too much of water add to it then it will be done and cooked/heat from the inside too everything will be perfect
  11. After its done off the cooker and serve the moi-moi on a flat plate and our Moi-Moi is ready
