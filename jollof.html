Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is one of the most popular food in Nigeria and every kids loves it especially when served with take away

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of White Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Spicy
  • Cory and thime
  • Nutmeg
  • Onions
  • 3 table spoon of Butter

Steps

  1. melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Turn on the cooker and place your pit on fire and add water, after that rinse the white rice in a bowl with water at least 2-3 times pour it inside the pot add maggi,salt,pepper,tomato paste,spicy,cory and thime,nutmeg and onions cooked and stired to make sure the ingredients are not stored in one place wait for 15-30 minutes and check if its soft enough to eat and turn off your cooker
  3. Serve it inside a plate or take away and our jollof rice is done
